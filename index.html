THE APP FOR RUSHING WALKERS

It sends you alerts while walking, when you need to hurry up or slow down to arrive on time
race the bunny and get a new character
The app is built as a game: racing the bunny for the carrot. As a reward for your punctuality you get a new character. Collect all the characters to get a printed version of them!
manage your walk to arrive on time
Get an alert when you are behind the bunny, so you need to hurry up or take public transit nearby. If you are really fast, the app sends you an alert to slow you down and suggests cafes nearby as a reward for your punctuality.
